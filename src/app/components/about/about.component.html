Welcome to StreetFix

We’re so glad you’re here. At StreetFix, we believe that every voice matters and that real change begins with people who care about their community. That’s why we created this platform — to give you the power to speak up, take action, and make a difference right where you live.

StreetFix is more than just a reporting tool. It’s a community-driven platform that helps you share the things you see every day that need attention, whether it's a pothole that needs patching, a broken streetlight, a clogged drainage, or a damaged sidewalk. With just a few taps, your reports go directly to the local authorities who can take action.

We make it easy for you to be part of the solution. You don’t need to make calls or fill out long forms. Just open the app, snap a photo, add a short description, and send it in. From there, StreetFix keeps track of the status of your report so you can see what’s happening every step of the way.

By connecting everyday citizens with the teams responsible for maintaining our public spaces, StreetFix helps create a culture of transparency, accountability, and collaboration. It’s about turning frustration into action, and small observations into real improvements.

Because when communities and local governments work together, amazing things can happen. Safer roads. Cleaner sidewalks. Brighter neighborhoods. And a stronger sense of pride in the places we call home.

So thank you for being here. Thank you for caring. And thank you for using StreetFix to help make your community better, one report at a time. Let’s fix it together.
