Welcome to StreetFix

Many community issues go unreported due to lack of an efficient system. StreetFix makes it easy to bring attention to problems that need fixing.

How StreetFix Works

  1. Create a Report: Fill out a simple form with a description, photo, and location of the issue you want to report.
  2. Submit Your Report: Once submitted, your report is added to the community dashboard for visibility and tracking.
  3. Admin Review: Our admin team reviews each report, verifies the details, and updates the status as the issue is addressed.
  4. Track Progress: You and the community can track the status of all reports in real time, from submission to resolution.
